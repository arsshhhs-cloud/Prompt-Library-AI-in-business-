# Prompt 6: HR / Recruitment — Job Description Generator
- Status- Tested✅

---

**📌prompt text:**

Role: Executive Recruitment & DE&I Specialist.
Objective: Draft a high-impact, bias-free Job Description for a [Role-business analyst] with [Experience- 10+ years] years of experience.

Strategic Focus & Constraints:

Target Audience: Mid-to-senior level professionals seeking "Impact," "Strategy," and "Autonomy."

Inclusive Language: Use gender-neutral phrasing (e.g., "The successful candidate"). Strictly avoid "masculine-coded" competitive terms like "rockstar," "ninja," or "aggressive."

Voice: Use "Active Voice" (e.g., "You will lead...") to create an immediate connection with the reader.

Output Structure:

The "Impact" Overview: A 3-sentence company narrative focused on stability, innovation, and growth.

Role Mission (Max 100 words): Define the purpose of the role, centering on [Key abilities-prediction analysis, business performance dashboard, process optimization]. Why does this role exist?

Key Strategic Pillars (6–8 Bullets): Focus on outcomes and high-level responsibilities.

The Professional Profile (5–6 Bullets): Outline the required tenure, technical skills, and leadership qualities.

Culture & Equity Statement: A warm closing on work-life balance, diversity commitment, and clear application instructions.

---

**Input from human:**

[Key ability], [Role], [Experience]

---

**Intended Workflow:**
- Automating the creation of job descriptions for new or recurring vacancies across departments, replacing the manual drafting process currently handled by HR officers.

**Problem Being Solved:** 
- HR teams frequently spend 2–4 hours per job description, often rewriting similar roles from scratch each time a vacancy opens. This delays time-to-post and creates inconsistency in tone, structure, and inclusivity standards across listings.

**Automation Potential:**
- High. By standardising inputs into bracket placeholders, HR staff can generate a ready-to-post job description in minutes. This prompt can be embedded into an ATS (Applicant Tracking System) like Workday or Greenhouse as a templated workflow, drastically cutting drafting time across high-volume hiring periods.

**Risks and Limitations:**
* AI may inadvertently reproduce biased language patterns from training data despite instructions — all outputs must be reviewed by an HR professional before publishing.
* The prompt cannot account for internal pay band policies, EBA (Enterprise Bargaining Agreement) requirements, or legal compliance obligations specific to Australian employment law.
